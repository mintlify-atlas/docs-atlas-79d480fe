Contafy offers flexible subscription plans designed to scale with your business needs, from freelancers to large enterprises managing high volumes of CFDI invoices.

## Available plans

Contafy provides four subscription tiers:

- **Free**: Ideal for freelancers just starting out
- **Básico**: Perfect for small businesses in growth
- **Pro**: Designed for accountants and accounting firms
- **Empresarial**: Built for high-volume operations and teams

## Pricing

All paid plans are available with monthly or annual billing. Annual subscriptions include a **20% discount** compared to monthly billing.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price (per month) | Annual Savings |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | $0 MXN | $0 MXN | - |
| Básico | $300 MXN | $240 MXN | 20% |
| Pro | $800 MXN | $640 MXN | 20% |
| Empresarial | Custom | Custom | Contact sales |

<Note>
Prices are in Mexican Pesos (MXN) and do not include VAT.
</Note>

## Trial period

Both **Básico** and **Pro** plans include a **30-day free trial** period. During the trial:

- Access all features of your selected plan
- No credit card charge until the trial ends
- Cancel anytime during the trial at no cost
- Automatic billing starts after trial completion

The Free and Empresarial plans do not include trial periods.

## Choosing the right plan

### Free plan

Best for:
- Freelancers and independent professionals
- Users processing up to 50 invoices per month
- Single RFC management
- Basic invoice validation needs

### Básico plan

Best for:
- Small businesses in growth phase
- Users managing up to 3 RFCs
- Processing up to 500 documents monthly
- Teams requiring PDF exports and complete reports

### Pro plan

Best for:
- Accounting professionals and CPA firms
- Managing multiple clients (up to 10 RFCs)
- Unlimited document processing
- Users requiring API access and Excel exports
- Priority support needs

### Empresarial plan

Best for:
- Large enterprises with high transaction volumes
- Organizations requiring unlimited RFCs
- Teams needing role-based access control
- Businesses requiring custom integrations
- Organizations wanting a dedicated account manager

The Empresarial plan requires direct contact with our sales team. Email us at **ventas@contafy.com** to discuss your specific requirements.

## Billing and payment

Contafy uses Stripe as the payment processor for all subscriptions:

- Secure credit/debit card processing
- Automatic monthly or annual billing
- Mexican Pesos (MXN) currency
- Proper CFDI invoice issued for all payments
