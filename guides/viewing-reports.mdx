Contafy provides powerful reporting tools to analyze your invoices, expenses, and cash flow. Generate visual reports, track trends, and make data-driven financial decisions.

## Dashboard overview

The main dashboard (`/dashboard`) provides a high-level view of your financial activity.

### Dashboard greeting

The dashboard greets you by name and displays the selected company (profile) or "Todas las empresas" if viewing all profiles.

### Accessing the dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Contafy">
    Navigate to `/dashboard` after logging in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a profile (optional)">
    Use the profile selector in the header to:
    - View a specific RFC profile
    - Select "Todas las empresas" for aggregated data
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the time period">
    Select:
    - **Mes**: Month to analyze
    - **Año**: Year
    - **Régimen fiscal**: Filter by tax regime (if applicable)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The dashboard automatically loads data for the current month and year when you first visit.
</Note>

## Financial metrics cards

At the top of the dashboard, four metric cards display key financial indicators.

### Total facturado (Total invoiced)

- **What it shows**: Sum of all invoice subtotals for the selected period
- **Includes**: PUE, PPD, and manual income entries
- **Excludes**: Payment complements (REP)

**Icon**: Blue card with trending up arrow

### Total de gastos (Total expenses)

- **What it shows**: Sum of all expense subtotals for the selected period
- **Includes**: XML expenses and manual expense entries
- **Excludes**: Invalid or rejected expenses

**Icon**: Purple card with shopping cart

### Diferencia (Difference/Net income)

- **What it shows**: Total facturado minus Total de gastos
- **Color coding**:
  - **Green**: Positive difference (profit)
  - **Red**: Negative difference (loss)
  - **Gray**: Zero difference

**Icon**: Trending up (profit) or trending down (loss)

### Facturas pendientes de pago (Pending invoices)

- **What it shows**: Number of unpaid PPD invoices
- **Includes**: PPD invoices with no payment or partial payment
- **Excludes**: PUE invoices (always considered paid)

**Icon**: Orange card with clock

<Tip>
  Click on any metric card to navigate to the detailed invoices or expenses page with the same filters applied.
</Tip>

## Flow trend chart

The flow trend chart visualizes income and expense trends over time.

### Chart features

- **X-axis**: Time period (months for yearly view, days for monthly view)
- **Y-axis**: Amount in MXN
- **Two lines**:
  - **Blue line**: Income (facturas)
  - **Purple line**: Expenses (gastos)
- **Interactive**: Hover over data points to see exact values
- **Responsive**: Adapts to screen size

### Viewing options

#### Año actual (Current year)

Shows monthly income and expenses for the entire year:
- January through December
- Each data point represents the month's total
- Useful for annual trends and seasonality

#### Mes actual (Current month)

Shows daily income and expenses for the selected month:
- Day 1 through last day of month
- Each data point represents a single day
- Useful for detailed cash flow analysis

#### Switch between views

Use the toggle buttons above the chart:
- Click "Año actual" for yearly view
- Click "Mes actual" for monthly view

<Note>
  The chart automatically updates when you change the profile, month, year, or régimen fiscal filters.
</Note>

## Recent invoices table

The dashboard displays the 3 most recent invoices.

### Table information

- **UUID**: Invoice unique identifier
- **Fecha**: Invoice date
- **Emisor**: Your business name
- **Receptor**: Client name and RFC
- **Total**: Invoice total amount
- **Estado**: Payment status (Pagado, No Pagado, Pago Parcial)

### Viewing all invoices

Click "Ver todas las facturas" to navigate to the full invoices list with detailed filtering and search.

## Recent expenses table

The dashboard shows the 3 most recent expenses.

### Table information

- **UUID**: Expense unique identifier
- **Fecha**: Expense date
- **Emisor**: Vendor/supplier name
- **Total**: Expense amount
- **Categoría**: Expense category badge (if assigned)

### Viewing all expenses

Click "Ver todos los gastos" to access the complete expenses list.

## Detailed invoice reports

The invoices page (`/dashboard/invoices`) provides comprehensive reporting for income.

### Invoice summary cards

At the top of the invoices page:

#### Total de facturas (Invoice count)

Number of invoices in the current filtered view.

#### Facturas pendientes de pago

Count of unpaid or partially paid PPD invoices.

#### Total facturado

Sum of all invoice subtotals (excluding payment complements).

### Invoice breakdown

The table shows detailed information for each invoice:

- **UUID / FOLIO**: Unique identifier
- **FECHA**: Invoice date and time
- **EMISOR**: Your business name and RFC
- **RECEPTOR**: Client name and RFC
- **MONTO**: Subtotal amount
- **IVA TRASL.**: IVA charged to client
- **RET. IVA**: IVA retention
- **RET. ISR**: ISR retention
- **ESTADO**: Validation and payment status
- **ACCIONES**: Delete button

### Manual income section

Below the summary cards, a separate section displays manual income entries (income without CFDI):

- **Concepto**: Description
- **Fecha**: Income date
- **Subtotal**: Amount before tax
- **IVA**: Tax amount
- **Total**: Final amount
- **Cobrado**: Payment status (Sí/No)
- **Acciones**: Edit and delete buttons

<Tip>
  Manual income entries are displayed separately from CFDI invoices for clarity. They contribute to financial metrics but don't have UUIDs.
</Tip>

## Detailed expense reports

The expenses page (`/dashboard/expenses`) offers comprehensive expense reporting.

### Expense summary cards

#### Total de gastos (Expense count)

Number of expenses in the current view.

#### Total gastado

Sum of all expense subtotals.

#### Categoría breakdown

If you've assigned categories, a pie chart or breakdown shows spending by category:
- Viáticos
- Oficina
- Servicios
- Transporte
- Alimentación

### Expense breakdown table

Detailed expense information:

- **UUID / FOLIO**: Identifier (or "Manual")
- **FECHA**: Expense date
- **EMISOR**: Vendor name and RFC
- **RECEPTOR**: Your business name and RFC
- **MONTO**: Subtotal
- **IVA TRASL.**: IVA charged
- **CATEGORÍA**: Category badge
- **ORIGEN**: XML or Manual
- **ESTADO**: Validation status
- **ACCIONES**: Edit (manual) or delete

## Combined financial report

For a complete financial picture, generate a combined report showing both income and expenses.

### Accessing the combined report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the dashboard">
    Go to `/dashboard` and select your desired filters (profile, month, year, régimen).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Exportar PDF'">
    In the top right of the dashboard, click the "Exportar PDF" button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for generation">
    The system generates a PDF report including:
    - Header with your business information
    - Financial metrics summary
    - Income breakdown (facturas pagadas y pendientes)
    - Expense breakdown
    - Net income calculation
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download the PDF">
    The PDF automatically downloads to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  PDF export is a premium feature available on PROFESSIONAL and ENTERPRISE plans. FREE and STARTER plans show an upgrade prompt.
</Note>

### Report preview

Before exporting, you can preview the report:

1. Click "Vista previa" instead of "Exportar PDF"
2. A new page opens showing the formatted report
3. Review the data for accuracy
4. Click "Exportar PDF" to download or "Volver" to go back

## Report filtering and customization

### By profile

Generate reports for:
- **A specific RFC**: Select the profile from the dropdown
- **All profiles**: Choose "Todos" to aggregate data across all your businesses

### By time period

Customize the report period:
- **Monthly reports**: Select month and year
- **Yearly reports**: Use the year selector and view annual trends
- **Custom periods**: Combine filters for specific date ranges

### By régimen fiscal

If your profile has multiple tax regimes:
1. Select the profile
2. Choose the régimen fiscal from the dropdown
3. Only invoices/expenses matching that régimen are included

<Tip>
  Generate separate reports for each régimen fiscal to simplify tax filing and accounting.
</Tip>

## Understanding payment status

Invoice and expense reports show payment status for better cash flow tracking.

### Invoice payment statuses

| Status | Description | When it applies |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **✓ Pagado** | Fully paid | PUE invoices or PPD with full payment |
| **No Pagado** | Unpaid | PPD with no payment complements |
| **Pago Parcial (X%)** | Partially paid | PPD with partial payment |

### Payment tracking

For PPD invoices:
- Upload REP (payment complement) XML files
- System automatically links REP to original invoice
- Payment status updates in real-time
- Progress is calculated based on paid amount / total

<Note>
  PUE invoices are always marked as "Pagado" since they represent single-payment transactions.
</Note>

## Best practices

### Regular review

- Check the dashboard weekly to monitor cash flow
- Review pending invoices and follow up with clients
- Compare monthly trends to identify patterns

### Accurate categorization

- Assign categories to manual expenses
- Use consistent naming for expense concepts
- Categorization improves report accuracy

### Export reports monthly

- Generate PDF reports at month-end
- Share with your accountant
- Keep copies for your records

### Monitor metrics

- Track the Diferencia (net income) metric
- Watch for increasing pending invoices
- Compare year-over-year growth

## Troubleshooting

### "No hay datos para mostrar"

No data exists for the selected filters. Solutions:
1. Expand the date range
2. Select "Todos" for profile
3. Remove régimen fiscal filter
4. Upload invoices or expenses first

### Chart not loading

The flow trend chart requires JavaScript. Ensure:
1. JavaScript is enabled in your browser
2. No ad blockers are interfering
3. Your browser is up to date

### Metrics seem incorrect

If numbers don't match expectations:
1. Verify the selected filters (profile, month, year)
2. Check if all invoices/expenses uploaded successfully
3. Review validation errors on uploaded files
4. Clear filters and reapply
